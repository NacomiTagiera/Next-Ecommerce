# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Next Sports. Our privacy policy is designed to protect your personal information.

## 2. Personal Data

### 2.1. What data do we collect?

- During registration: First name, last name, email address.
- When making purchases: Shipping information, order history.
- Automatically while using the website: IP address, browsing data.

### 2.2. How do we use your data?

- To process orders and deliveries.
- To contact you in case of any order issues.
- For data analysis to customize our offerings to your preferences.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties without your consent unless required by law.

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data.
